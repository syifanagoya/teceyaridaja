Welcome along to today's Qatar 2022 World Cup fixture wherein Croatia and Canada face off in group F at the Khalifa International Stadium, in the city of Al Rayyan. The referee for this match is Andrés Matonte (URU). The two assistant referees will be Nicolas Taran (URU) and Martin Soppi (URU), and the VAR official is Mauro Vigliano (ARG).

Croatia vs Canada: Follow the World Cup match live on November 27

Update narration

On November 27, Croatia and Canada will face each other to try to reach the last 16 of the Qatar 2022 World Cup. We've got all of the information you need to follow the game.

In which stadium will the World Cup fixture between Croatia and Canada be played?

The match between Croatia and Canada will be played at the Khalifa International Stadium in the city of Al Rayyan. The Khalifa International Stadium is one of the eight stadiums hosting 2022 World Cup matches. It has a capacity of 40,000 spectators. The match for third place in this World Cup will be played in this stadium.
