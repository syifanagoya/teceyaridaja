Suzette Quintanilla is an American actress and former drummer of the band named Selena y Los Dinos. She is best known as the elder sister of Selena, who was one of the most prominent entertainers of the late 20th century. The Mexican-American singer was shot dead on March 31, 1995, two weeks before her 24th birthday.

Suzette with her siblings, Abraham III and Selena formed the band called, Selena y Los Dinos. Currently, Quintanilla works to preserves her late sister's legacy. Suzette was born on June 29, 1967, in Lake Jackson, Texas, the United States. Middle of three children, she was born to Abraham Quintanilla Jr. (father) and Marcella Ofelia Samora Quintanilla (mother). Her parents have been married for 57 years.

Tragic Death of Sister

The Queen of Tejano music, Selena Quintanilla was shot and killed on March 31, 1995. She was just 23 years old. Yolanda Saldivar, who was Selena's friend and her former manager shot Selena in a motel room at the Days Inn in Corpus Christi. Yolanda surrendered after she failed to flee up the scene.

Despite being a friend, Yolanda began misusing Selena's trust and became a bad influence. Additionally, she had Selena's bank and financial records. She will be eligible for parole on March 30, 2025.

Selena was buried at Seaside Memorial Park in Corpus Christi, Texas. Although she has gone for more than 25 years, Selena is still very popular for her music. She is one of the most influential Latin artists of all time.
