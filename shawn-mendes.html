Shawn Mendes Biography Timeline

Shawn Mendes is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and model who rose to fame as a teenager through his videos on the social media platform Vine. Known for his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics, Mendes has become one of the biggest pop stars in the world, with a loyal fan base and numerous hit songs to his name.

1998: Born on August 8 in Toronto, Canada.

2013: Began posting covers of popular songs on Vine, quickly gaining a large following and attracting the attention of record labels.

2014: Signed with Island Records and released his first single, “Life of the Party”.

2015: Released his debut album “Handwritten”, which debuted at number one on the charts and spawned the hit singles “Stitches” and “Treat You Better”.

2018: Released his self-titled album “Shawn Mendes”, which includes the hit singles “In My Blood” and “Lost in Japan”.

2019: Became the youngest artist to headline Wembley Stadium in London.

Shawn Mendes continues to captivate audiences with his emotional performances and honest lyrics, solidifying his status as one of his generation’s most talented and successful musicians.
